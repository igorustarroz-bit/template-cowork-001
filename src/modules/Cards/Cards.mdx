# Cards

## Intro

Full-width module of three cards for highlighting products or content. One
card is **expanded** (wider, with a description) and the other two are
**collapsed**. Figma: `Cards` (component set variants
`State=Collapsed/Expanded`).

## Anatomy

1. **Header** — section title + `ActionButton` (primary, S) aligned right.
2. **Card row** — 3 cards in a 12-column grid.
3. **Card** — background image + an overlaid **panel** (bottom) with a
   title, description (only when expanded) and an `IconButton`
   (terciary, S).

## Sub-themes

The **module's own theme** is controlled by the global toolbar selector.
Each **card** accepts its own `theme` (`data-theme` override) that overrides
the module's — e.g. module in *Dark · Red Primary* with panels in
*Light · White*. See the `ModosMezclados` story and the convention documented
in `CONTEXT.md`.

## Behavior

- **Only one card is expanded at a time.** Clicking a collapsed card expands
  it and collapses the previous one (single-selection).
- The button icon reflects state: **−** (expanded) / **+** (collapsed).
- Expansion is animated via `flex-grow` + `ease-out`.
- _Detected from the Figma `State=Collapsed/Expanded` component-set variants._

## Variants and sizes

| State | Width | Content |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Expanded | 6 columns (`grow-[2]`) | title + description + icon − |
| Collapsed | 3 columns (`grow`) | title + icon + |

Responsive 12-column grid; the three cards add up to 6 + 3 + 3.

## Accessibility

- Every card is interactive and exposes `aria-expanded`.
- The `IconButton` carries an `aria-label` ("Expandir/Colapsar …").
- Native visible focus (`:focus-visible`) on cards and buttons.
- Contrast depends on the active mode; verify AA when combining nested modes.
